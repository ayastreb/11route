Düsseldorf, Germany

March 30, 2014
Spring has came to Dusseldorf, it's +21C and sunny outside. We started our walk from Dusseldorf-Derendorf.
We lived in this neighborhood for about a month when we moved in to Germany.
Derendorf is a nice place to live, but it took about 1 hour to get to our office from there.

Then we headed to Altstadt and visited Hofgarten park.
