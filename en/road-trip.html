Road Trip, The Beginning

August 31, 2009
At the end of the summer I found out that my cousin is coming to San-Francisco with a friend and they want to go on a road trip around west side of the USA. I decided to joined them, we rented a car and headed on. We left San-Francisco and drove towards Seattle. On our way we visited Muir Woods National Park.

Some huge trees are growing there.

Stopped on the beach.

It was too cold to swim, but the view is nice.

Stopped overnight in a motel near Medford, OR.

The next day we moved on.
