Salzburg, Austria

August 2, 2016
We bought our tickets on the bus company website FlixBus, arrived to the train stations (usually buses also depart from train station or from nearby), and waited for our bus. But it didn't come because as it turned out we've bought train tickets We had to buy new tickets and wait 2 more hours for the next train.
We arrived in Salzburg at the evening. We didn't have much time so we just went for a short walk in the city center.

Interesting factory building.

Salzburg is the birthplace of Mozart, everything here has a music theme.

On the main street we met street musicians playing classical music. They were quite good

House where Mozart was born.

On the Mozartplatz we met another street band called StreetWhispers. They are playign very good jazz, so good that one lady couldn't hold it and started to dance

The smallest house in Austria.

The whole shop of Mozartkugeln candies.
