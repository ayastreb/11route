Cannes, France

May 11, 2016
From Nice we moved to Cannes, it's just half an hour train ride. We arrived in Cannes one day before the start of the film festival. The preparations for the main event of the year were going on everywhere. We didn't make it to the festival because our flight was on the next day and regular people are not allowed there anyway, it is only for film industry professionals.

Photographers are taking places.
