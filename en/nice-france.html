Nice, France

May 9, 2016
After returning our car to Venice Airport we went to Nice by train. The trip is pretty long and takes almost the whole day - around 8 hours in the train.
We arrived to Nice in the evening, settled in the hotel and went to sleep after the dinner. On the next day morning we went for a walk around the city. In the morning it was cloudy, but in the afternoon the sun came out.

In the city center the are no wires and trams are running on batteries.

Interesting children playground.
